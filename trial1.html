Obscure Feelings

Nikhedonia

the feeling of excitement or elation that comes from anticipating success.

Alysm

the feeling of restlessness or frustrated boredom that comes from being unwell.

Misslieness

the feeling of solitariness that comes from missing something or someone you love.

Lonesome-Fret

that feeling of restlessness or unease that comes from being on your own too long

Malneirophrenia

the feeling of unease or unhappiness that comes from waking up from a nightmare.
